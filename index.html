Project Overview

Electric Vehicles (EVs) rely on efficient charging systems to replenish battery energy. This project compares AC and DC Fast Charging methods based on charging speed, efficiency, battery degradation, infrastructure requirements, and cost.

Results & Findings

  • DC Fast Charging significantly reduces charging time.
  • AC Charging offers better long-term battery health.
  • DC charging infrastructure is more expensive.
  • Both methods have specific use cases.

Conclusion

AC Charging is ideal for daily charging and battery longevity, while DC Fast Charging is best for rapid charging requirements and long-distance travel.
